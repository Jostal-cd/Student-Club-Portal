GDSC

President: Angela Dsouza

GDSC CRCE is a student-led developer community that focuses on helping students ideate, build, and ship real-world tech solutions through hands-on events and projects. The club runs hackathons (like Bit n Build), founder retreats (Unplug), tech workshops, design sessions, and flagship events such as Future Forge to grow skills across software development, product thinking, UI/UX, and data. It also provides a structured council (GDSC GRID) where members take up roles in tech, management, design, marketing, and outreach, creating a culture of collaboration, ownership, and community impact on campus.
